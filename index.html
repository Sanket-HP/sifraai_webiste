About Sifra AI

Sifra AI is the world's first autonomous data scientist tool, developed by Indian student Sanket Sarjerao Patil. He is also the author of the book "The 1% Theory: Building Success from Belief", inspired by Virat Kohli's philosophy. Sanket is pursuing a B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering with a specialization in Data Science. His research paper on Sifra AI is published on TechRxiv and can be read at: TechRxiv.org.

Mobile App – MVP Stage

Currently in the MVP stage, available only as a mobile app (not yet published). It uses a custom GPT model hosted on Microsoft Azure and is supported by the Microsoft for Startups Founder Hub. The frontend is built using Flutter and Android Studio.
